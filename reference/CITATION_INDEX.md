## Confirmed Citations

### 1. Haghighi et al. 2022 — Cross-modal gene expression + morphology
- **Title:** High-Dimensional Gene Expression and Morphology Profiles of Cells across 28,000 Genetic and Chemical Perturbations
- **Authors:** Haghighi M, Caicedo J, Cimini BA, Carpenter AE, Singh S
- **Journal:** Nature Methods, 2022, 19:1550-1557
- **DOI:** 10.1038/s41592-022-01667-0
- **PMC:** PMC10012424
- **Use:** A2 anchor. Cross-modal concordance baseline (shared vs complementary subspace framing).
- **Status:** VERIFIED. PDF not downloadable (paywall), read via PMC.

### 2. Funk et al. 2022 — Phenotypic landscape of essential genes
- **Title:** The phenotypic landscape of essential human genes
- **Authors:** Funk L, Su KC, Ly J, Feldman D, Singh A, Moodie B, Blainey PC, Cheeseman IM
- **Journal:** Cell, 2022, 185(24):4634-4653.e22
- **DOI:** 10.1016/j.cell.2022.10.017
- **PMID:** 36347254
- **Use:** Experiment C anchor. Essential gene knockouts produce measurable, clustered phenotypes. >90% of 5,072 fitness gene knockouts have measurable phenotypes.
- **Status:** VERIFIED. PDF not downloadable (paywall).
- **CORRECTION:** Perplexity lists "Itzhak et al. (PubMed, 2022)" as a separate citation — this is a hallucination. No such paper exists. The description matches Funk et al. exactly.
