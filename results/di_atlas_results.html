Covariatesn_contexts (1)

The hypothesis was wrong in direction. Genes essential in more cell lines (DepMap Chronos < −0.5) have lower morphological DI, meaning their knockout phenotypes are more conserved across contexts. This makes biological sense: essential genes have constrained, conserved functions, so their loss produces similar morphological disruption regardless of cell type. The CI is entirely negative and the effect is well-powered (p ≈ 10−171), so this is a genuine negative result, not underpowered.
